Our Services

Threading Services

Threading in CNC machining involves creating precise screw threads on both internal and external surfaces of a workpiece. Our advanced CNC equipment ensures high precision and repeatability, making it ideal for producing fasteners, machinery components, and automotive parts.

Boring Services

Boring is the process of enlarging a pre-drilled hole with high accuracy. Our CNC boring services provide precise internal diameters and smooth finishes, perfect for creating custom-fit components in industries like automotive, aerospace, and heavy machinery.

Parting Services

Parting, or cutting off, involves separating a part from the main stock material. Our CNC parting services ensure clean, accurate cuts, minimizing material waste and ensuring that the final product meets your exact specifications.
